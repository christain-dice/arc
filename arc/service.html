OUR SERVICE

The company's building portfolio includes some of the most iconic structures across Nigeria, especially in Abuja where state of the art buildings is redefining the cityscape.

Dallas Conzut has the capacity to deliver both designs and build, as well as turnkey buildings. From administration, commercial and industrial buildings, hotels, hospitals, sports facilities to single homes or entire residential districts.

OUR AREA OF EXPERTISE

Full Architectural Services

Where a client chooses to use our full range of architectural services from start-to-finish of a project, our work is carried out in stages, from preliminary feasibility studies through to the developed technical design and on to the construction and completion of the building works.

We also advise on the need to appoint other appropriate consultants, and on the best way to appoint a contractor to suit the particular requirements of each client and project.

Dallas Conzut has specialized know-how in the design and construction of sustainable and eco-friendly buildings that meet the Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) standards for certification.

Concept & Feasibility Design

Planning & Listed Building Applications

We are highly experienced and successful in dealing with the often complex and sensitive planning process.

We apply a knowledgeable and careful approach to the preparation, submission and negotiation of all types of planning and listed building applications, including in conservation areas and for subterranean developments.

On projects within the high-end residential sector and for private clients, we offer an in-house interior design service. This can include the full design of bathrooms and kitchens as well as joinery and lighting design and the selection of finishes and fittings.
